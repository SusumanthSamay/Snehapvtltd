About Us

Three Decades of Expertise in Security Services

Creating Opportunities in Security Sector

SNEHA Security Services Pvt. Ltd. brings three decades of expertise in security management, providing comprehensive training and employment solutions for youth across Andhra Pradesh.

Our project aims to identify unemployed youth, provide them with industry-standard security training, enhance their technical and practical skills, certify them upon completion, and facilitate job placements in various security-related fields.

We are committed to creating a sustainable pipeline for job-ready candidates in high-demand security roles while enhancing community safety and supporting youth employment.
